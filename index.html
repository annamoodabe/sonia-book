Red and Yellow,
Round they go.
Circles and dancing
How entrancing!
Green and Blue, the planets trace
Their rotating paths through space
Harsh Moustache and slithering snakes? That is simply frightful!
Sticking worms and ocean waves? Thats much more delightful!
Coloured curves
And oblongs make
A magic carpet or mosaic.
Nestled, shining,
Near the middle,
A rare black marble- what a riddle!
Join me for a treasure hunt
In Africa’s wild continent.
Searching for the crocodile -
Can you see his eyes and smile?
And those other wriggling shapes,
Do you think they might be snakes?
Purple moon and its eclipse,
Yellow sun
And chalky cliffs…
What a thrill it is to fly
In this plane,
Across the sky!
Round the red
Propeller races.,
Taking us
To far-off places
Don’t these planets in suspension
Catch your eye and your attention?
What a journey - it’s been fantastic
And, thanks to Sonia Delaunay,
So artistic!
